# VIBA Credit Model 2026

## Competitive reference

Current builder-app references checked on 2026-06-22:

- Replit Core is listed at $25/month monthly or $20/month annually, with $25 monthly credits.
- Replit Pro is listed at $100/month monthly or $95/month annually, with $100 monthly credits.
- Bolt Pro is listed at $25/month monthly, with 10M tokens/month.
- Lovable uses a credit system, grants free daily build credits, and charges credits according to task complexity.

## VIBA target model

- Membership: $50/month.
- Trial: 3 days.
- Trial credits: 260 credits total.
- Monthly included credits: 500 credits.
- Top-up pack: 500 credits for $50.
- Credit renewal: monthly allowance resets each billing period.

## Usage estimates

| Workflow | Estimated credits |
|---|---:|
| Project review and written findings | 180-260 |
| Small repair | 60-120 |
| 2-3 small repairs | 180-300 |
| Full review plus multi-step repair cycle | 550-700 |

## Product rule

The trial must be useful enough to prove value, and it must be disclosed as an evaluation allowance. It should not be presented as enough for a full production repair cycle.

When credits run out, the app should pause the session and offer two choices:

1. Buy 500 credits for $50.
2. Wait until the monthly allowance renews.

## Disclosure copy

```txt
Your trial includes 260 credits, enough for discovery work such as a project review with findings or several small repairs. Larger full-fix workflows may require a paid membership, a 500-credit top-up, or waiting until your monthly credits renew.
```
