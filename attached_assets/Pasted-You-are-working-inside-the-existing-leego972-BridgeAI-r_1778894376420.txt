Logic:
- grammar cleanup, simple classification, sentiment = high reliability
- response comparison, factuality, coding = medium reliability
- math, expert domain, subjective judgment = low or medium depending on rubric clarity
- missing rubric = lower confidence and careful review
- expert domain always requires human review
